How to Delete Your Account

Your privacy and control over your data is important to us. If you'd like to delete your account, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Open the App on your mobile device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings or Profile section.
  3. Scroll down to the Account section.
  4. Tap on the "Delete Account" button.
  5. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.

Once confirmed, your account and associated data will be permanently deleted from our system.

Note: This action is irreversible. Make sure to back up any important information before deleting your account.

If you need help, contact us at support@yourapp.com
